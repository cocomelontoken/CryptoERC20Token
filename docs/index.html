CoComelonToken

On September 1, 2006, Cocomelon was created on YouTube under the username "checkgate", a month before Google acquired YouTube


2013: The channel rebranded to "ABC Kid TV" and began remastering older videos, transitioning from alphabet videos to nursery rhymes.

2016: The channel transitioned to 3D animation, with the first 3D video being released.

2018: The channel rebranded to Cocomelon and introduced a recurring cast of characters.

2021: Cocomelon was purchased by Candle Media (formerly known as The Chernin Group) for a reported $3 billion.

Present: Cocomelon has expanded globally in multiple languages, with spinoff series, Netflix TV shows, merchandising, special events, and ongoing product diversification.

Cocomelon has become one of the most-subscribed and most-viewed channels on YouTube, known for its engaging and educational content for young children
